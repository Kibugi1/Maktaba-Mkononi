Welcome!

Welcome to Maktaba Mkononi. We are an online platform that allows you to share books with people from your local area.

Maktaba Mkononi enables pople to read physical books in the absence of a public library. It allows people to share physical books with each other and broaden the reading scope of the community by pooling books together.

Login to enjoy a seamless infrastracture and happy reading!
